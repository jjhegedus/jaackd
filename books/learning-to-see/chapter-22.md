# Chapter 22 — The Aftermath


It was late when they got back. Three in the morning. But they were still rolling and not ready for sleep. They played until it got light out and kept playing. Laughing and talking about who would be next. Paul was ready to go now but Kevin just laughed and finally booted him out about nine in the morning saying he needed some sleep.

Paul was still too wired so he decided to take a walk. He knew where he would end up this time.

He walked into the pool hall just after eleven when they opened.

This time he saw him as soon as he walked in. Sitting there in his chair in the corner already. Not asleep this time but eating a burger.

Paul got a set of balls from the counter and grabbed a table nearby, glancing over in his direction. He still didn't know his name, not that it mattered. He threw the balls out on the table and started making them. With authority.

It wasn't long and he heard the familiar voice. "You look tired and excited at the same time. Good night?"

"The best," Paul replied.

And that was all he needed. He gathered the balls up, returned them to the counter and walked home to get some well earned rest.

Until next time.
